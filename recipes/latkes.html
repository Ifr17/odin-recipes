Latkes

Description

A traditional Ashkenazi Hanukkah treat!

Superior to sufganiyot :p

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Yellow Onion
  • One egg
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • All-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. Grate potatoes and onion into a large bowl
  2. Mix in egg, salt, and pepper. Add enough flour to make the mixture thick
  3. Preheat the oven to low, about 200 degrees F
  4. Heat 1/4 inch oil in the bottom of a heavy skillet over medium-high heat
  5. Drop in 2-3 lumps of batter, and flatten with back of a spatula
  6. Fry, turning pancakes once, until golden brown
  7. Transfer pancakes to a plate lined with a paper towel to drain. Keep warm in the oven until serving. Repeat until all potato mixture is used.
