Awesome Broccoli-Cheese Casserole

Description

A tasty and cheesy dish to make your veggies unhealthy!

At least your kids will eat it.

Ingredients

  • Can of condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • Mayonnaise
  • One egg
  • Chopped onion
  • Broccoli
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350f and butter a baking dish
  2. Whisk condensed soup, mayonnaise, egg, and onions together in a bowl until combined
  3. Place frozen broccoli into a very large mixing bowl and break it up if necessary; add soup mixture and mix well to coat. Sprinkle with cheese and mix well; spread mixture into the prepared baking dish. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes to 1 hour
